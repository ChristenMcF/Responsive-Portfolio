About Me

I see myself as a student of life, and since I never stop learning or discovering, I've amassed a cornucopia of random (possibly useless) trivia over the years. As a self professed coffee snob, I view the art of making a strong cup of joe a necessary life skill. I have an affinity for good bourbon, sarcastic wit, and anything with a skull and crossbones motif. I am a trail ultrarunner, adventurer, and music junkie with a passion for photography and writing. Sometimes, all at once. I would love to be the lead singer of a band, but that requires more talent than just "sounds really good in the car". I find my serenity on the trails of the Cuyahoga Valley National Park, and usually have my camera in tow. I plan on completing my first 100 mile race within the next two years, and look forward to publishing a book of my photography.
